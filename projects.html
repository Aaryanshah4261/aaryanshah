Structural and Thermal Evaluation of Electronic Power Conditioner (EPC) Unit for Space Payload

– Designed three iterations of an EPC unit for microwave space payloads using Autodesk Inventor.
– Conducted comprehensive structural and thermal simulations (modal, quasi-static, random response, steady-state, and transient analyses) using Ansys Workbench.
– Performed DFMEA to identify potential failure modes, mitigating structural and thermal risks.
– Executed topology optimization to reduce weight by 47% while maintaining integrity.
– Presented final design recommendations based on FEA outcomes for improved thermal management and structural reliability.
