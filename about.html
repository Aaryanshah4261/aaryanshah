Who I am

Hello, I am a Master's student in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Michigan, specializing in finite element analysis (FEA), thermal and structural simulations, and advanced product design. With hands-on experience in industry and academia, I have developed a strong foundation in mechanical engineering principles, complemented by a proven ability to optimize designs and streamline processes.

Finite Element Analysis and Mechanical Design


I have a strong background in Finite Element Analysis (FEA) and Mechanical Design, with experience in structural, thermal, topology optimization and crashworthiness simulations. I have worked on designing and analyzing packages, electronic components, space payloads, robotic mechanisms, and automotive safety systems using software like Ansys Workbench, LS-Dyna, HyperMesh, and SolidWorks. My work includes topology optimization, Structural simulation, thermal simulation, parametric modeling, and material analysis, leading to significant improvements n weight reduction, structural integrity, and performance optimization. Through various internships and projects, I have developed expertise in CAD modeling, FEA simulation, automation, and design optimization, applying these skills to solve real-world engineering challenges.
